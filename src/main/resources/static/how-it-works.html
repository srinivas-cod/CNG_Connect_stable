About CNG Connect

The Problem

CNG vehicle owners in Chennai often face anxiety regarding fuel availability. Long queues, stations abruptly running out of stock, and a lack of real-time information make refueling a time-consuming and frustrating experience.

Our Solution

CNG Connect bridges this gap by providing a real-time, community-driven platform. We connect drivers with live station data, ensuring you know exactly where to go and what to expect before you even start your engine.

Key Features

📍

Smart Radius Search

Automatically detects your current location and scans for stations within a 5-20km radius.

🟢

Real-Time Status

Live updates on station status: Available, Queue, or Out of Stock.

🛣️

Optimized Routing

Get the fastest route with estimated travel time and distance using advanced mapping.
