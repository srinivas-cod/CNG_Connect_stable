Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

Welcome to CNG Connect. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide to us directly and information that is collected automatically when you use our service:

  • Personal Information: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and authentication details via Google Firebase.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our map, such as which stations you view and reports you submit.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we access your device's geographical location to provide real-time navigation and show nearby CNG stations.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Provide and maintain our service (e.g., showing near stations).
  • Authenticate your account and secure your data.
  • Improve our "Live Pulse" queue estimation technology.
  • Communicate with you regarding updates or support.

3. Data Protection & Security

The security of your data is a priority. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols provided by Google Firebase to protect your personal information.

We NEVER sell or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website utilizes several third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication and secure database management.
  • OpenStreetMap/Leaflet: For displaying maps and routing information.
  • Render/GitHub: For hosting and deployment operations.

These services have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.

5. Intellectual Property Protection

All data generated by the community (station status reports) and original algorithms used for "Total Time to Refill" (TTR) calculations are the property of CNG Connect and its creator, Srinivas S.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. You can do this through your account settings or by contacting our support team.
